# Beginner Reading and Viewing List

This page is for newcomers. It prioritizes gentle explanations before research papers.

## Start here

### Hugging Face - Introduction to Vision Language Models

<https://huggingface.co/learn/computer-vision-course/en/unit4/multimodal-models/vlm-intro>

Why: beginner-friendly explanation of VLMs inside a computer vision course.

### Hugging Face - Vision Language Models Explained

<https://huggingface.co/blog/vlms>

Why: practical intro to how VLMs work, model selection, inference, and fine-tuning.

### Chip Huyen - Multimodality and Large Multimodal Models

<https://huyenchip.com/2023/10/10/multimodal.html>

Why: clear conceptual framing of multimodality, CLIP, Flamingo, and LMMs.

### OpenAI - CLIP: Connecting text and images

<https://openai.com/index/clip>

Why: original high-level explanation of why CLIP mattered.

### Pinecone - Multi-modal ML with OpenAI's CLIP

<https://www.pinecone.io/learn/series/image-search/clip>

Why: practical explanation of multimodal embeddings and image search.

### Lightly AI - A Brief Introduction to Vision Language Models

<https://www.lightly.ai/blog/introduction-to-vision-language-models>

Why: approachable evolution from CLIP to Flamingo and LLaVA.

## Beginner-friendly YouTube

### LLaVA: A large multi-modal language model

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=q59QMugQyaQ>

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BcfAkQagEWU>
